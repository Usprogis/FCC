Friedrich Nietzsche

God is dead. God remains dead. And we have killed him.

Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche was a German philosopher, cultural critic, composer, poet, philologist, and Latin and Greek scholar whose work has exerted a profound influence on modern intellectual history.

Time line of Nietzsche life:

  • 1844 - born and grew up in Röcken, Prussia (nowadays known as Lützen, Germany).
  • 1854 - he began to attend Domgymnasium in Naumburg.
  • 1858 - transfered to Schulpforta and studied there until 1864.
  • 1864 - graduates at Schulpforta and commenced studies in theology and classical philology at the University of Bonn.
  • 1869 - becomes professor of classical philology at the University of Basel in Switzerland.
  • 1879 - becomes an independent author in different cities.
  • 1889 - sufferes a mental breakdown.
  • 1900 - dies at the age of 55 in Weimar, Germany.
